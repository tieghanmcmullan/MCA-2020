Task 1: Generating a jSymbolic of my musical piece

For this task, I have generated a JSymbolic of my piece. I picked out the features that I feel are important and interesting when looking at my piece.
Below is a list of the features I have included:

  • Number of Pitch Classes: 8
  • Number of Pitches: 26
  • Strong Tonal Centres: 2
  • Range: 42
  • Pitch Variablity: 10.89
  • Mean Pitch Class: 5.157
  • Mean Pitch: 58
  • Most Common Pitch Class: 7
  • Most Common Pitch: 48
  • Most Common Melodic Interval: 2
  • Interval Between Most Prevalent Pitches: 4

I have extracted the main and most basic features about the piece such as number of pitches and pitch classes, mean pitch and mean pitch class, as well as Range. I have also extracted some features which I believe can provide more information about this song such as strong tonal centres, most common pitch and pitch class, the interval between the most prevalent pitches, pitch variability and most common melodic interval. These features will allow me to learn more information about my piece. For example, the most common melodic interval tells me that the most common distance between notes in the melody of this piece is 2. Furthermore, the most common pitch can give me insight into how high or low the piece of music is.
