Histograms computed from chromagrams

Chromagrams are often referred to as "pitch class profiles", they are used for analysing music where pitch can be categorised into 12 pitch classes. This is I have made sure to show 12 histograms here. Chromagrams capture harmonic and melodic characteristics of music but are also able to identify changes in timbre and instrumentation. The differences which have been vsualised in the histograms show how different each song is.
