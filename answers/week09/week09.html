A: Computing and visualising histograms

Histograms computed from MFCCs

Mel frequency cepstral coefficients are a set of 20 features that combine to describe timbre, below are three sets of 20 histograms - 1 for each feature.

Breathing In Awareness Head Ain't Cheating

Histograms computed from chromagrams

Chromagrams are often referred to as "pitch class profiles", they are used for analysing music where pitch can be categorised into 12 pitch classes. This is I have made sure to show 12 histograms here. Chromagrams capture harmonic and melodic characteristics of music but are also able to identify changes in timbre and instrumentation. The differences which have been vsualised in the histograms show how different each song is.

Breathing In Awareness Head Ain't Cheating

B: Comparing the histograms

For this part of the task, I am comparing histograms for the MFCC feature. This is usually used to analyse timber i.e the tone colour or tone quality so should give an idea of genre. Due to the fact the tracks I am comparing are each different genres and feature different instruments, the histograms computed from the mfcc data should show this difference and they do.

I would say the histograms for "Breathing" and "Head Ain't Cheating" are slightly similar in shape from number 8 onwards. Whereas "In Awareness" has a very different shape and most of them except 0-3 are very similar. All three sets of histograms are distinct from each other, feature 0 in each song is totally different.
